Camp Facilities

If you already have decided to come to the summer camp, and also already contacted with us, you should read the following information about what you should bring, and also not bring.

    If you are coming to the School of Youth program you should bring.

  • Tent (not important, we can give you one if you don´t have one).
  • Comfortable shoes for rock climbing.
  • Light and comfortable clothes, as well as some fast drying towels.
  • A very important object to you.
  • You could also bring a book or two.
  • There´s no Internet conexion at the valley, so you coud bring your phone but it will be useful.
  • And the most important thing, make sure to bring some happiness and good vibes, in this program we will help you with anything.
